Reading these: Tap the direction switch to see it respond. Sliders are functional. These are layout mockups — colours, typography, and control shapes are the point, not pixel-perfect fidelity to the actual plugin.

What's not mocked: Collapsible sections, hover/focus states, long-press tooltips, the help overlay, multi-stroke interaction, multilane colour coding, incoming MIDI ghost display. Those are second-pass conversations once a direction is chosen.

Planned elements not shown: Piano-key root/mask picker (shown only in #6), collapsed scale row, future Random direction segment, multilane lane-colour swatches.
